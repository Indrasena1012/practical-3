Welcome to Karnavati University

Welcome to Karnavati University

Karnavati University, located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, is a premier institution dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and academic excellence. Known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure and industry-aligned curriculum, the university offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to prepare students for the modern world.

Computer Science at Karnavati University

The Unitedworld Institute of Technology (UIT) at Karnavati University offers cutting-edge programs in Computer Science, including BSc (Hons.) in Computer Science, BCA (Hons.), and BTech in Computer Science and Engineering. These programs are tailored to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving tech industry. Students can specialize in advanced fields such as:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML) - Dive into intelligent systems and predictive analytics.
  • Data Science (DS) - Master the art of extracting insights from big data.
  • Cybersecurity - Learn to protect systems and networks from digital threats.
  • Core Computer Science - Build a strong foundation in programming, algorithms, and software development.

With hands-on projects, internships, and access to advanced labs, our programs ensure students are job-ready. Check your timetable and results below to stay updated!

About Us

Karnavati University is a vibrant academic hub committed to holistic education. Established with a vision to empower students with knowledge and skills, we blend theoretical learning with practical exposure. Our faculty comprises experienced educators and industry experts, ensuring a dynamic learning environment. The university also boasts modern facilities, including a Wi-Fi-enabled library, advanced sports amenities, and a lush green campus.

Our Courses

Explore our diverse range of Computer Science programs:

BSc (Hons.) in Computer Science - A 3-year program focusing on theoretical and practical aspects of computing, with specializations in AI/ML and Data Science.

BCA (Hons.) - A 3-year course emphasizing application-oriented skills in software development and IT services.

BTech in Computer Science and Engineering - A 4-year engineering degree with specializations in AI/ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Core CS, designed for hands-on technical expertise.
